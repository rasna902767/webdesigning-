✦ What is Devcation?

Creative Design Track:
Devcation Revamp

Devcation Revamp is a creative design track where participants use AI-powered tools and prompt engineering to design the official Devcation Hackathon page.

Participants will be provided with the current Unstop page as the base reference. Using prompt engineering and AI tools, they must redesign the website by creating a modern UI layout, improving visual hierarchy, enhancing the user journey, and proposing one original innovative feature.

Examples of innovative features: Interactive timeline, countdown timer with milestone reminders, or an interactive FAQ chatbot assistant. The final design should clearly communicate the hackathon's theme, information, and excitement.

✦ Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Who can participate?

Open to all college students across India — any stream, any year. No prior ML or AI experience required. Just bring your creativity!

What is the team size?

Teams can be 1–4 members. Solo participation is fully allowed. We recommend diverse teams combining design, dev, and creative thinking.

What AI tools can we use?

Any publicly available AI tool — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Midjourney, Copilot, Stable Diffusion. Focus is on your prompt engineering quality, not the specific tool.

Is there a registration fee?

Completely FREE. Zero registration fee. Sign up on Unstop and you're in. All you need is curiosity and an internet connection.

What are the submission requirements?

Submit: (1) GitHub repo + deployed link, (2) A doc listing 3–5 key prompts used, (3) Screenshot of your landing page.

How will we be judged?

Criteria: Creativity & Innovation (30%) · Prompt Quality (25%) · Technical Execution (25%) · UI & Presentation (20%).
